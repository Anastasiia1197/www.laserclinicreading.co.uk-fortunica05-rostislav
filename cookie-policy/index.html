Unlocking better experiences: How cookies work on https://lottiesdogs.co.uk/

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. At https://lottiesdogs.co.uk/, cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings for future visits
  • Understand how you navigate our website to improve its structure and content
  • Ensure the security of your connection and protect against fraudulent activity
  • Provide you with relevant content and offers tailored to your interests
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns in the UK market

Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information unless you have voluntarily provided it. They cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device. We categorise cookies based on their function and lifespan, which we detail in the sections below.

Take control: How to customize your cookie preferences easily

You have full control over the use of cookies on your device. Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings. Here’s how you can exercise control:

  • Browser Settings: You can usually find cookie settings in the "Options", "Tools", or "Preferences" menu of your browser. Common browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge all provide options to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored.
  • Opt-Out Tools: For third-party advertising cookies, you can visit websites like Your Online Choices (UK) to opt out of targeted advertising from participating companies.
  • Our Consent Banner: When you first visit https://lottiesdogs.co.uk/, you will see a cookie consent banner. This allows you to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customise your preferences before continuing to browse.

Please note that disabling certain cookies, especially essential ones, may affect the functionality of our website. Some features may not work as intended, and your browsing experience could be less personalised and efficient.
