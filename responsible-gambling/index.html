Identifying Warning Signs Before Problems Arise

Self-awareness is crucial. Problem gambling often develops gradually. Regularly ask yourself honest questions about your behaviour and feelings towards gambling, regardless of whether you're playing at Fortunica Casino or elsewhere.

Key warning signs to be mindful of include:

  • Spending more time or money on gambling than you originally intended, perhaps while chasing a win or trying out a new Fortunica promo code offer.
  • Chasing losses—trying to win back money you've lost by gambling more or increasing your bets.
  • Thinking about gambling constantly or feeling restless when not playing.
  • Borrowing money, selling possessions, or lying to family and friends to fund gambling or hide losses.
  • Neglecting work, studies, family responsibilities, or personal relationships due to gambling.
  • Gambling to escape problems, or feelings of anxiety, depression, or helplessness.

Recognising one or more of these signs in yourself is a strong indicator that it's time to pause, reassess, and seek further information or help.

Debunking Common Gambling Myths and Misconceptions

Separating Fact from Fiction

Misconceptions about gambling can lead to risky behaviour. Understanding the reality is a key part of playing responsibly at any casino, including Fortunica Casino.

Myth: "I'm due for a win after a long losing streak."
Fact: Each game round on a certified UKGC slot is completely random and independent. Past results do not influence future outcomes. The notion of being "due" a win is a fallacy known as the Gambler's Fallacy.

Myth: "Betting systems can beat the odds and guarantee profits."
Fact: No strategy can overcome the built-in house edge in games of chance. Systems like Martingale (doubling bets after a loss) are risky and can lead to rapid, significant losses, especially with volatile games. This applies to all games at Fortunica Casino.
